About Wheelhub

A few summers ago I bought my first car, a 2003 Mitsubishi Lancer OZ Rally. It was an awesome car, but I needed wheels to mount the winter tires that came with it. The first set I bought were some second-hand black steel wheels. The center bore, width and offset were all wrong.

Thanks to some help from a good friend and some research I got it right with the next set, but I wished I had something that could easily calculate fitment differences between wheels to make wheel shopping easier.

So, I built Wheelhub. It's a way to compare new wheels and/or tires with your car's OEM setup. You enter your OEM specs, and then see in real-time how different wheel offsets, widths, and tire sizes would affect your car's setup.

Give it a shot!
